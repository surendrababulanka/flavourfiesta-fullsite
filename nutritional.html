Fresh Salad

A delicious and nutritious fresh salad.

  • Rich in Vitamins and Minerals: Vitamin A, C, K, Folate (B9), Potassium.
  • Low in Calories: Excellent for weight management.
  • High in Fiber: Aids digestion and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Hydrating: High water content from ingredients like cucumbers and lettuce.
  • Antioxidants: Protects from oxidative stress and inflammation.

Information:

  1. Calories: 15-30
  2. Vitamin A: 2000-2500 IU
  3. Vitamin C: 10-15 mg
  4. Vitamin K: 60-80 mcg
  5. Folate: 50-60 mcg
  6. Potassium: 200-300 mg
  7. Fiber: 1-2 grams

Fruit Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy fruit smoothie.

  • Oranges and strawberries provide vitamin C, which boosts the immune system and aids in skin health.
  • Spinach and kale offer vitamins K and A, which support bone health and vision.
  • Bananas are high in vitamin B6, which supports brain function and mood regulation.
  • Avocados provide vitamin E, which acts as an antioxidant to protect cells from damage.

Information:

  1. Calories: 11-20
  2. Vitamin A: 2000-2700 IU
  3. Vitamin C: 06-15 mg
  4. Vitamin K: 60-80 mcg
  5. Folate: 30-60 mcg
  6. Potassium: 100-300 mg
  7. Fiber: 0-4 grams
