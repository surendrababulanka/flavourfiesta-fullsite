Beef Wellington


  • 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Information:


  • Gather all ingredients.
  • Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  • Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved.
    Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat.
    Refrigerate for at least 2 hours .
  • Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  • Season with salt and garnish with coriander leaves.
  • Serve and enjoy!.
